Books are the treasured wealth of the world and literary inheritance of generations and nations.

The School boasts of two well stocked Libraries catering to the needs of the junior, Middle and senior school.
Each class is allotted one period in the weekly timetable for the to issuance of books. However, the library is open to both the staff and students who can make use of the Reference section of the library.
The Library has a Reading room where periodicals and well known dailies are available.
